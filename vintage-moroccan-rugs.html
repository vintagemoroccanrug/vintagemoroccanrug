  • Spot clean stains promptly using a mild soap solution and a soft cloth.
  • Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading of colors and patterns.
  • Rotate the rug every few months to ensure even wear.
  • Use a rug pad to provide cushioning and reduce movement on hard floors.
  • Store the rugs in a cool, dry place when not in use, wrapped in cotton fabric.
  • Consult a professional cleaner experienced with vintage textiles for deep cleaning when necessary.
