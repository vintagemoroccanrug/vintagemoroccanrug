FAQS

What should I expect from a Moroccan rug workshop?

In a Moroccan rug workshop, you can expect to learn about traditional weaving techniques, the history of Moroccan rugs, and hands-on experience in creating your own rug under the guidance of skilled artisans.

Are the workshops suitable for beginners?

Yes, most Moroccan rug workshops cater to all skill levels, including beginners. They provide step-by-step instructions and support to help you create a beautiful piece, regardless of your prior experience.

How long do the workshops typically last?

The duration of workshops can vary, but most sessions last between 2 to 4 hours. Some workshops may also offer multi-day courses for a more in-depth experience.

Can I purchase a rug after completing a workshop?

Yes, many workshops offer the opportunity to purchase rugs made by the artisans or even your own creation at the end of the session. This way, you can take home a unique piece that you helped create.

What materials will I be using during the workshop?

Participants will typically use natural materials such as wool, cotton, and dyes derived from plants. The workshops emphasize traditional methods, ensuring an authentic experience while crafting your rug.
